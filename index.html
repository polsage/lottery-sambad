Is Nagaland State lottery real?

Yes, Nagaland State Lottery is 100% real. It is owned and regulated by the Government of Nagaland, established in 1972 under the Lotteries (Regulation) Act, 1998. The draws are conducted transparently under government supervision, and results are published officially in PDF format with complete winner details.

How much time will it take to get money after winning the lottery?

After winning a prize, it takes approximately three days to receive the prize money after submission of all required documents along with the original winning ticket to the Nagaland State Lottery office. For prizes above ₹10,000, a government-issued ID proof is mandatory.
